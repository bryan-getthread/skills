# Escalation Prep

**When to use:** A ticket needs to move to a higher tier, a TAM, or a vendor, or a technician wants an escalation recommendation.

**What you get:** A structured internal note covering the situation, what has been tried, and what is needed, plus the status and board change.

## Steps

1. Walk the escalation checklist, one question at a time if information is missing.
2. Summarize the issue, environment, impact, and everything already attempted with results.
3. State clearly what the next tier needs to do and why it is being escalated now.
4. Post the formatted internal note, then set the target board, status, and priority.
5. Where appropriate, recommend whether it should become a project or change request instead.

## Guardrails

- Do not escalate on a thin note. Require the checklist to be complete first.
- Confirm the target board and assignee before moving the ticket.

## Consolidates

Escalation requested, escalation summary/briefing, escalate-to-tier-2, escalation advisor, and escalation prep skills.
