# Client Health Report

**When to use:** An account manager wants a weekly or ad-hoc read on a client's support activity and risk signals.

**What you get:** A client-ready summary with the period's volume, top issues, SLA performance, and two or three concrete recommendations.

## Steps

1. Confirm the client and date range. Default to the previous week.
2. Report ticket volume and breakdown by type and priority versus the prior period.
3. Identify the top recurring issues and any noisy assets or users.
4. Cover SLA performance and anything breached.
5. Give two or three concrete recommendations (training, a fix, a project) to reduce volume.

## Guardrails

- Write for the account manager or client, not in internal jargon.
- If volume exceeds what one query returns, say so and give an order-of-magnitude estimate rather than a false-precise number.

## Consolidates

Weekly client health report, client health scan, and client ticket health-check skills.
