# My Queue Summary

**When to use:** A technician asks what is on their plate, what to work next, or for a summary of their open tickets.

**What you get:** A prioritized list of the technician's open tickets, each with its state and a recommended next step.

## Steps

1. Pull all open tickets assigned to the requesting technician.
2. For each, give a one-line state and the recommended next action.
3. Rank by priority, SLA risk, and age.
4. Highlight the single most important thing to work next and why.

## Guardrails

- Scope strictly to the requesting technician's own tickets.
- Lead with the top priority rather than a flat list.

## Consolidates

My assigned tickets summary, technician ticket review, priority-of-the-day, and 'what should I work next' skills.
