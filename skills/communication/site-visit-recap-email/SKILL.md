# Site Visit Recap Email

Draft the recap a client receives after a technician's onsite visit: a clear account of what was done, what is now working, and what is still outstanding with owners and timing. It turns a visit into a documented, reassuring update.

## When to use

- "We were onsite at <client> today — draft the recap."
- "Write up what the tech did on the visit."
- A field/onsite ticket has visit notes and the client needs a summary.

## Steps

1. Read the ticket and the tech's visit notes/time entries before writing. Base the recap only on what is recorded — do not embellish work that isn't documented.
2. Open with a one-line context anchor: the visit date and the reason for the visit.
3. Cover, in short sections:
   - **What we did** — the work performed on site, in plain language.
   - **Resolved** — what is confirmed working now.
   - **Still outstanding** — anything not finished, why, and who owns the next step (with a date if one is recorded).
   - **Recommendations** — optional, only if the tech noted them; frame as suggestions, route anything billable to the account manager rather than committing.
4. If a follow-up visit or part order is needed, state it plainly with the expected next step — do not invent a date that isn't set.
5. Structure and tone per the Email Baseline Standard (communication/email-baseline-standard).
6. Present the draft with view_openDraft for review. Do not send.

## Guardrails

- **Only documented work.** Every action in the recap must trace to the ticket/visit notes. No invented steps, parts, or timings.
- **No new commitments.** Don't promise a return visit date, a fix time, or billable work that isn't confirmed — flag those as `NEEDS:` for the tech or AM to confirm.
- Never blame the client's environment, a vendor, or a prior tech; state facts.
- Degradation: view_openDraft is in-app only. Over external MCP, output the draft in chat labeled "DRAFT — review before sending."
- See scheduling-and-dispatch/field-visit-scheduler for booking the visit — this skill is the post-visit recap only.
