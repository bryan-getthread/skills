# Employee Offboarding

**When to use:** An employee is leaving and their access needs to be shut down and reclaimed on the effective date.

**What you get:** A completed offboarding checklist with each destructive step confirmed, and a summary of anything needing manager or HR sign-off.

## Steps

1. Confirm the user and effective date. Treat every step as security-sensitive.
2. Disable sign-in and reset the password immediately.
3. Revoke active sessions and MFA methods.
4. Convert or forward the mailbox per policy and set an out-of-office.
5. Remove licenses and group/app access; reclaim or transfer shared resources.
6. Note hardware for return, disable managed devices, and summarize what needs sign-off.

## Guardrails

- Confirm before each destructive action. Do not run the whole sequence unattended.
- Follow client policy for mailbox retention and data handoff.

## Consolidates

Employee offboarding and offboarding checklist skills.
