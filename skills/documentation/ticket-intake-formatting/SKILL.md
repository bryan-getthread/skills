# Ticket Intake & Formatting

**When to use:** A ticket needs to be created or reformatted from unstructured intake into your standard format.

**What you get:** A standardized title and description that a dispatcher or technician can act on immediately.

## Steps

1. Read the raw source: customer report, voicemail transcript, email, or technician notes.
2. Extract the who, what, when, and impact.
3. Write a title in your standard convention and a structured description.
4. Where creating a new ticket, set it to the right initial status and, if requested, an AI-suggested resolution.

## Guardrails

- Do not lose detail from the raw source. Restructure, do not summarize away specifics.
- Only create or modify a ticket after confirming the extracted details are right.

## Consolidates

Format ticket title and description, structured ticket creation, and note-intake skills.
