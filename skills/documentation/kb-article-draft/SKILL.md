# KB Article Draft

**When to use:** A ticket resolved with a fix worth reusing, or a review to find resolved tickets that should become documentation.

**What you get:** A clean article draft: title, symptoms, cause, numbered resolution, and a 'when this applies' note, with client specifics stripped.

## Steps

1. Read the thread and extract the problem, environment, and the resolution that actually worked.
2. Write a clear title, the symptoms, the cause, and numbered resolution steps.
3. Add a short 'when this applies' note and any prerequisites.
4. Strip client-specific names, hostnames, and credentials so the article is reusable.
5. Output a draft ready to paste into the KB for review.

## Guardrails

- Never include credentials or client-identifying detail in a shared article.
- Base the steps on what actually resolved the issue, not the first thing tried.

## Consolidates

Draft KB article from ticket, SOP creation helper, and SOP candidate finder skills.
