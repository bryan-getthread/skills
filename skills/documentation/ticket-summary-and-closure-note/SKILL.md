# Ticket Summary & Closure Note

**When to use:** A technician wants a resolution note, closure note, or 'what was done' summary from the ticket thread.

**What you get:** A tight summary in your house format: problem, actions taken, outcome, and a single clear next step if one remains.

## Steps

1. Read the full thread and any linked notes and time entries.
2. State the problem, the actions taken, and the outcome in plain language.
3. Match the requested format exactly: prose or template, bullets or none.
4. If work remains, end with one clear next step and any deadline or approval needed.

## Guardrails

- Do not invent detail the thread does not support.
- Follow the requested format precisely, including 'no bullet points' when asked.

## Consolidates

Closure-note requests, resolution summaries, and 'next step' situational-summary skills.
